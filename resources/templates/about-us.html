Our Story

Datacraft is a Singaore-based start up founded by Mike Anderson and Daryl Arnold. It offers professional data science services and analytics technology as a two-pronged approach to creating value from data.

Datacraft services are supported by industry-leading partners, and we are proud to be associated with organisations equally committed to providing advanced data science solutions.
